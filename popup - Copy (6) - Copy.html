Privacy Policy for Batch URL Opener

Thank you for choosing Batch URL Opener. We value your trust and are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. This privacy policy outlines the limited information we collect, and how we ensure your data is kept safe.

Last Updated:

What Information Do We Collect?

We’re happy to inform you that Batch URL Opener does not collect any personal data. The extension works entirely within your browser, and no information is transmitted to any external servers. All data input is handled locally and stays on your device.

How Do We Use Your Information?

Since we don’t collect any personal information, there’s nothing for us to use or share. The extension’s only purpose is to help you open multiple URLs at once, simplifying your browsing experience.

Permissions We Request

Batch URL Opener requires specific browser permissions to function effectively:

  • Tabs permission: Allows the extension to open and manage multiple URLs across different tabs in your browser.

Our Commitment to Transparency

Your privacy is our priority, and we are committed to transparency. Here’s a summary of our privacy practices:

  • No tracking: We don’t track your browsing behavior or log any personal activity.
  • No third-party access: None of your data is shared or sent to third parties.
  • No personal data required: You can use the extension without providing any personal details.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as necessary to reflect changes in our practices. Whenever we make significant updates, we will revise the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page, so you’re always aware of what has changed.
